About the Machine.

The Machina Emblematica is a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system, an AI that combines vector-based search with natural language responses generated by a Large Language Model. It draws directly from the 1668 edition of Symbola et Emblemata, using scanned images provided by the Münchener Digitale Bibliothek, and transcriptions of the text.

Ask the machine anything you'd like to know about the Symbola – its contents, topics, imagery, sources, or symbolism. The Machina will search the original work, look up relevant passages, and reply in modern language. No need to be fluent in Latin or Greek. Though if you are, the Machina will love the opportunity to converse in either!

A Word of Caution.

The Machina has been worn down by centuries of use. Don't trust everything it says. Its responses may contain errors, gaps, or wrong interpretations. Use it as a helpful guide, but stay skeptical and consult the original sources where needed.
